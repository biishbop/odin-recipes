Triple Pigs in Blankets

Description

Pack in three times the flavour with these triple pigs in blankets. You can prepare them a day or two in advance and keep on a baking tray in the fridge

Ingredients

  • miniature chipolatas
  • 8 rashers smoked streaky bacon
  • 1 tbsp sunflower oil

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 200C/180C fan/gas 6. Pile the sausages in groups of three and wrap a piece of bacon around each trio.
  2. Arrange on a baking tray and brush or drizzle with the oil. Roast in the oven for 30-35 mins until the chipolatas are golden and the bacon is crisp.
